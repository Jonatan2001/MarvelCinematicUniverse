MCU Movies

Plot of the MCU Movies


Phase 1

Iron Man

After, the egotistical and self-obsessed billionaire weapons designer Tony Stark has been kidnapped, by terrorists he espaces by inventing the Iron Man suit, seeing the error of his way, he returns to America, shuts down his weapon manufacturing company and further enhances the Iron Man suit to battle terrorism.



The Incredible Hulk

After exposed to high amounts of Gamma rays, Dr. Bruce Banner is desperately trying to find a way to reverse it's effects and destroy the uncontrollable Hulk, on his journey he reconnects with his old love, and faces off with old and now foes.



Iron Man 2

Now that the world has gained knowledge about the Iron Man, Tony Stark is under pressure to share his technology with the world and if that wasn't enough he is slowly being killed by the very device that is meant to keep him alive. Can he figure out how to solve these problems, with his best friend James "Rhodey" Rhodes, War Machine and with Pepper Potts along with the mysterious Nick Furry, the head of S.H.I.E.L.D and Natasha Romanoff, before time runs out?



Thor

After foolishly refusing the command of his father and his king and starting war, Thor is banished to Earth, without his powers the thousands of years old diety, must learn humility and stop the war and his evil borther Loki.



Captain America: The First Avenger

It's 1941, although he wants nothing more then to join the millitary, Steve Rogers can't due to health issues, until he is given an opportunity to become the ultimate super soldier and becomes Captain America, after which he helps the allied forces to help defeat Nazi-backed HYDRA, with his best friend Bucky Barnes and Peggy Carter.



Marvel's The Avengers

When Thor's evil brother, Loki gets hold of the Tesseract a powerfull object that could help him travel through space and will allow him to open a portal to help him unleash his armies on Earth, knowing the threat couldn't be handled by them Nick Furry inlists the help of Tony Stark/Iron Man, Steve Rogers/Captain America, Thor, Burce Banner/Hulk Natasha Romanoff/Black Widow and Clint Barton/Hawkeye, to help them defeat Loki. The real question is: can they learn how to act like a team in time, before all is lost?



Phase 2

Iron Man 3

After the events in New York, Tony Stark is struggling with PTSD and he tries to compensate for that, by building as many Iron Man suits as possible to try to protect Earth, his girlfriend, Pepper Pots and himself. However all of that is jeopardized when a new villian The Mandarin tries to kill Tony and all of his loved ones. Joining him in the fight is his best friend James "Rhodey" Rhodes/Iron Patriot.



Thor: The Dark World

After returing to Asgard with his brother, Loki to answer for his crimes Thor once again returns to Earth to find Jane Foster, who has been accidently been exposed to the Ether, a deadly force that is also being searched by the Dark Elfs, who want to use it to destroy the Nine Realms.



Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Steve Rogers has difficulty adjusting to his new reality and when his boss Nick Furry is shot dead, he is forced to go on the run to uncover the corruption in S.H.I.E.L.D, with Black Widow and Sam Wilson/Falcon, but that might not be so easy as a mysterious assassin, known as the Winter Soldier is trying to stop them. Steve can stop him, but the real question is will he?



Guardians of the Galaxy

After Peter Quill/Star Lord finds the mysterious Orb, he gets into trouble and he is thrown into jail along with the talking tree Groot who can only say "I am Groot", along with the assassin Gamora and with the talking Rocket Raccoon, where they meets Drax the Destoyer, they quickly escape though and they take the Orb to the Collector only to find out that it's an Infinity Stone and that the evil Ronan, is after it who wants to use the Stone to destroy a whole planet. Will this band of misfits be enough to stop him?



Avengers: Age of Ultron

Afer recieving a vision of a terrible future, Tony Stark creates a global peace keeping program, named Ultron, to protect Earth from danger. However Ultron misunderstands his purpose and decides that the only way peace can be achieved is by killing all life on Earth, now the Avengers must once again join their forces to stop him. But can they? And is stopping him will be enough to repair the damage that has been done, not to Earth but to the Avengers?



Ant-Man

Scott Lang, is recruited by Hank Pym to be Ant-Man, a person that has all the abilities and the size of an ant, to stop Darren Cross, to unleah his own shrinking supersuits, the Yellowjacket on the world, in the form of HYDRA.



Phase 3

But when the dust is settled will there be a winner?

Dr. Strange is a self-obsessed man, who only caress about himself and his carer. After an accident prevents him from continuing his job, he goes on a journey to discover himself and the mystic powers.

Now that he got a taste of superhero life, Peter Parker want nothing more than to become a full fledged Avenger, but Tony Stark believes that he isn't ready for that. So Peter goes rouge and causes mayhem on his way, so much so that Iron Man, needs to intervene and takes back the suit. After the death of their father, Thor and Loki find out that they have a sister who shortly after meeting them destroys Mjolnir and banishes them to a different world. Joining them is Valkyrie, who has grown tired of loosing her loved ones and is in a self imposed excile, can they defeat Hella together and at what price?

Avengers: Infinty War

The Titan Thanos, has started his quest to seek out the 6 Infinity Stones and on his way he runs into Thor and the Hulk, who manages to escape in time to warn the now torn apart Avengers, but before Tony and Steve could reconcile, the Children of Thanos arrive and take the Time Stone and Doctor Strange, who is saved by Tony and Peter, and they are later joined by some of the Guardians of the Galaxy, meanwhile on earth, Steve Rogers, Natasha Romanoff, Wanda Maximoff, Sam Wilson, Bruce Banner and Vision join forces with Wakanda to protect the Mind Stone and along with it half of the whole universe.



Ant-Man & The Wasp

After the events, of Civil War, Scott Lang is placed under house arrest for two years, and he didn’t stay in touch with Hank and Hope, but a few days before the end of his sentence he gets a dream of Janet van Dyne, from the Quantum Realm and before he knows he is out from his house in search of Janet and trying to out run a group of people who are desperate to get their hands on Hank’s technology and Ghost, who wants to get her hands on Janet, so she could save her before she dies.



Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers, has been lost in space and in her lack of memories for 6 years on an alien planet, until she accidently returns to Earth... in 1995. In this Blockbuster of a movie she reconnects with her old self, and some old friends and she gains a couple of new ones in the form of the young Nick Furry, who still has both of his eyes and the shape shifting Talos a Skrull alien who is looking for a new home for his people.



Avengers: Endgame

After the devastating ending of Infinity War, the remaining Avengers along with the help of Captain Marvel, Nebula and Rocket seek out Thanos, the Infinity Stones and justice, but they fail as Thanos destroyed the Stones and all hope is lost. 5 years later, however a new hope arises and Scott Lang returns from the Quantum Realm with interesting news; they could use the Quantum Realm to travel back in time to get the Stones. At first Tony is reluctant to go ahead with Scots proposed time heist, as now he has a daughter and it takes his love and guilt over Peter to convince him. As the Avengers travel back in time however, the past version of Thanos gain knowledge of their plan and comes to an even more terrifying conclusion then before.
